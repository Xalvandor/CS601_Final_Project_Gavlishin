All About Me!

I was born in Ukraine. I moved to the US when I was 4 years old. I grew up in the Philadelphia area.

In college, I decided to study music and music education. I played classical guitar for recitals, weddings, and other events.

After college, I lived in France for 2 years where I taught English in public elementary schools.

After returning to the US, I became an ESL teacher in Philadelphia.

Seeking a change and wanting to further challenge myself, I decided to pursue a Master's in Software Development at BU.
